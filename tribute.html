Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Introduction:

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, India. Known as a wicket-keeper batsman. Nicknamed "Captain Cool" and "Mahi." Played for India and Chennai Super Kings (CSK).Selected for the Indian national cricket team in 2004

Captaincy Achievements:

Led the team to victory in the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in 2007. India achieved the number one ranking in Test cricket under his leadership in 2009. Guided India to win the 2010 Asia Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup
